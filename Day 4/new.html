About Pravah

PRAVAH is the Annual Cultural, Technical and Sports Festival of Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology (SKIT), Jaipur. Experience seven exciting days filled with competitions, innovation, cultural performances, workshops, sports and unforgettable memories.

7-Day Schedule

Day 1 (17 Feb)

  • Opening Ceremony
  • Coding Contest
  • Quiz Competition
  • DJ Night

Day 2 (18 Feb)

  • Hackathon
  • AI Workshop
  • Robotics
  • Poster Presentation

Day 3 (19 Feb)

  • Cricket
  • Football
  • Chess
  • Volleyball

Day 4 (20 Feb)

  • Dance Battle
  • Singing
  • Fashion Show
  • Open Mic

Day 5 (21 Feb)

  • Photography
  • Painting
  • Rangoli
  • Treasure Hunt

Day 6 (22 Feb)

  • Gaming Tournament
  • Battle of Bands
  • Stand-Up Comedy
  • Celebrity Night

Day 7 (23 Feb)

  • Prize Distribution
  • Cultural Finale
  • Closing Ceremony
  • Fireworks
